About Our School

Our School Introduction

Established with a vision to empower girls through quality education, our English medium institution has a rich legacy built step by step:

  • 1979 – Foundation laid with Priti’s Playgroup and Shri D.D. Meghani English Nursery & R.H.T.T Pre Primary Section
  • 1980 – Smt. R.N. Sheth Ratnachandraji English Primary Section established
  • 1984 – Smt. S.T. Mehta English Medium Secondary Section established
  • 100% results consistently achieved for more than 35 years

School offers quality education at affordable fees, with:

  • Spacious, well-lit smart classrooms
  • Highly qualified teachers and a favorable student-teacher ratio
  • A well-stocked library
  • Science and computer labs with advanced coding training
  • A spacious auditorium and music education
  • Transport facilities
  • Free counseling and guidance
  • A wide range of sports — basketball, chess, gymnastics, mallakhamb, taekwondo, athletics, and table tennis — under professional trainers
  • CCTV surveillance for a safe and enriching learning environment

Our school’s Aim

To provide holistic and quality education that promotes academic excellence, character development, emotional well-being, and lifelong learning with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in life and make meaningful contributions to society.

Our school’s Vision

To provide quality education that nurtures global citizens and future leaders through the values of empathy, discipline, integrity, and respect, by integrating modern technology, innovation, and holistic learning.

Our school’s motto

Educate • Empower • Excel
