Welcome to the official website of Smt. S. T. Mehta English Medium Secondary Girls' School. As you explore our pages, you’ll get a glimpse into the vibrant and dynamic life of our school from academic excellence to a wide array of co-curricular activities. Every achievement, every event, and every milestone reflects the journey of our students, guided by a passion for learning and a commitment to growth.

We truly believe, as the saying goes, that “The mind is not a vessel to be filled but a fire to be kindled.” With this philosophy at our core, we strive to spark curiosity, foster creativity, and instill strong moral values in our students. Our dedicated team of educators work tirelessly to shape young minds into confident, compassionate, and responsible individuals—ready to face the world with resilience and purpose.

Our pride lies in the unwavering dedication of both our students and staff, as seen in our school’s remarkable record — 36 consecutive years of a 100% pass rate in the SSC examinations. Beyond academics, we focus on holistic development through enriching activities like sports, and instrumental music.

We invite you to stay connected with us and be a part of the spirit of learning, discovery, and excellence that defines Smt. S. T. Mehta English Medium Secondary Girls’ School.
