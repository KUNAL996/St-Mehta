Preschool education is like the first colourful stroke on a blank canvas, laying the foundation for a child's lifelong journey. We at Priti’s Playgroup & D.D. Meghani Nursery & Shri. R.H.T.T Pre-Primary understand the immense importance of preschool education and its profound impact on a child's growth and development. Hence we provide an environment for children to explore, imagine and express themselves freely.

Through play- way based activities, theme days, role play, stories, art and songs, we help children develop not just basic skills, but also confidence, curiosity and compassion. The main aim is to develop communication and interactive skills through drama techniques.

Our dedicated teachers guide each child with patience and love, ensuring that learning becomes a natural and delightful process. I also extend my heartfelt gratitude to all the KG parents for their support, involvement and trust which means the word to us.

“Children are like flowers in a garden – each unique, each blooming at their own pace , adding beauty, colour and fragrance to our school family” Thus together let us nurture their roots with love, water their minds with knowledge and provide them the sunshine of encouragement so they grow into confident, compassionate and capable individuals
