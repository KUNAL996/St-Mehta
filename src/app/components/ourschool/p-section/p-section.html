Mrs. Kirti S Pansare

Principal, Smt. R.N. Sheth Ratnachandraji Primary Section

(M.A. D.Ed. D.S.M.)

It’s truly a privilege to serve as the Principal of Smt. R.N. Sheth Ratnachandraji Primary Section. Every day, I’m inspired by the bright smiles, boundless energy, and curious minds of our young learners.This early stage in a child’s life is so important, and we take great care in laying strong academic foundations while also nurturing values that will guide them for life.

Our school is not just about textbooks and lessons — it’s a warm, welcoming space where girls are encouraged to ask questions, show kindness, and be proud of who they are. We believe in helping each child grow into someone who is not only intelligent, but also compassionate and confident.

Through creative teaching, engaging activities, and a supportive environment, we help our students explore their strengths and discover their potential. Whether it's in the classroom, on the playground, or during co-curricular activities, we make sure every experience adds to their personal and academic growth.

At R.N. Sheth, we believe that every girl deserves a strong and joyful beginning to her educational journey. With this belief at heart, we remain committed to shaping thoughtful, well-rounded individuals who are ready to shine in whatever path they choose.
